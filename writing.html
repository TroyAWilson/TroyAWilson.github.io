~Writing~

Come on, sit down.

I have ideas for stories, primarily short horror stories inspired by a mix of creepypastas/internet horror I enjoy and some works by HP. Lovecraft and those who have made addition to the lovecraftian mythos.

Currently, however, I have nothing truley concrete to share. I do have two loose ideas I want to document here that I hope to one day make into full stories.

Idea 1: Too Many Faces [tentative title]

This story follows a young man who lives in a mountain town named Sycamore Falls who starts to notice sometimes people aren't quite right.

"I high-fived Peter in celebration. That's when I noticed his face seemed to waver--slipping, settling, and reforming like he couldn't quite remember which expression fit"

"The sound it made when it spoke was my voice, almost. It was a tone that did not pass through lungs but through something vast and hollow imitating the human throat"

Idea 2: Garden at the Edge of WestWell

A story told in the form of journal entries written by a Horticulturist living deep within an abandoned garden at the edge of a forgotten town.

The entries slip further into madness as the garden's secrets unravel.

"Vines bleed clear luminous fluid that smell of honey and rust."

"I dreamed again woke with soil under my nails. I find myself longing for the next dream"

"To lie down in her warmth is to return to the first thought of creation -- to become seed and song and soil again. Do not come looking for me, only her. And she will welcome you too."
